About Me

Akanksha Sharma is an internationally acclaimed CSR & Sustainability Leader, a Writer and a Sustainable Fashion Curator and a Model.

She is noted amongst '100 Most Impactful CSR Leaders Globally' by World CSR Congress and ‘Young CSR Leader’ by India CSR in 2016. She is also conferred with “Sustainability Leadership Award” by the World Sustainability Forum.

She is also a ‘Global Shaper of the World Economic Forum’. Akanksha is also a “Global Climate Force Ambassador” and was chosen to represent India on “International Expedition on Climate Change at Antarctica” in 2017.

She practices ‘Zero-waste living’ and is a vegan by choice. Born and brought up in Jaipur, Rajasthan, her spirit is fuelled by her pursuits of travelling, star gazing and creating new memories!

EXPERIENCE


After completing her Master’s in Business Administration, she has immensely contributed in the CSR domain with her 10 years of experience:

  • She currently heads CSR and Sustainability for Jubilant FoodWorks Ltd.

  • She has worked with UNICEF and Vedanta Resources in the past

  • Successfully led community development programs focusing onPoverty Alleviation, Skill Development, Nutrition, Education, Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, Health & Sanitation, Farmers Development, Road Safety, Peace Education and others at remote rural and urban areas of India and Srilanka.

  • Helped organizations partner with various ministries and policy making bodies for responsibly driving Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) by developing a Resource Convergence Mechanism on CSR.

 

Member & Contributor:

  • National CSR Expert with the Indian Institute of Corporate Affairs, Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Govt. of India
  • Member of Confederation of Indian Industries (CII) CSR Forum
  • Committee Member for United Nations Global Compact CSR and SDGs Linkage Forum

Specialization:
Akanksha has done her specialization in Social and Environmental Business Management from World Bank Institute. She has also done an advanced course in Social Investment from UK Social Investment Forum.

Advisor:
Her role with the private corporations is to advise Boards and Leadership on Responsible Business, CSR & Sustainability along with developing organizational approaches to establish strategic partnerships and institutional engagement for sustainable development.
